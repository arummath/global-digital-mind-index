# 📚 Global Digital Mind Index: EU Source Directory (2026)

This directory contains the primary and secondary sources used to analyze the European Union's AI governance framework, focusing on the "Personhood Gap" and liability attribution.

## ⚖️ I. Primary Legislative Texts
*These are the foundational laws that define AI as a "Product" rather than an "Agent."*

* **[The AI Act - Regulation (EU) 2024/1689](./data/EU_Sources/OJ3AEN_TXT.pdf)**
    * *Core Focus:* Risk-based classification, Article 3(1) machine-based definition, and Article 14 Human-in-the-loop requirements.
* **[Digital Omnibus on AI: Implementation Simplification (March 2026)](./data/EU_Sources/ST-7322-2026-INIT_en.pdf)**
    * *Core Focus:* Latest updates on enforcement timelines (delayed to 2027/2028) and administrative simplification for SMEs.
* **[EU Guidelines on Prohibited AI Practices (Article 5)](./data/EU_Sources/Guidelines_on_prohibited_artificial_intelligence_practices_established_by_Regulation_EU_20241689_AI_Act_English.PDF)**
    * *Core Focus:* Detailed interpretation of unacceptable risks (Social Scoring, Manipulative AI) grounded in Human Dignity.
* **[Council Proposal on Implementation (Nov 2025)](./data/EU_Sources/ST-15708-2025-INIT_en.pdf)**
    * *Core Focus:* Initial proposals for the "Digital Omnibus" and the rationalization of GPAI obligations.

## 🏛️ II. Institutional & Policy Frameworks
*Official documentation from the European Commission and OECD regarding global cooperation and liability.*

* **[AI Liability Directive - Legislative Train Status](./data/EU_Sources/_a-new-plan-for-europe-s-sustainable-prosperity-and-competitivenessai-liability-directive.pdf)**
    * *Key Insight:* Records the **withdrawal** of the dedicated AI Liability Directive, highlighting a major gap in non-contractual fault attribution.
* **[OECD: Progress in Implementing the EU Coordinated Plan (Vol 2)](./data/EU_Sources/3ac96d41-en.pdf)**
    * *Key Insight:* Analysis of AI uptake in high-impact sectors (Health, Manufacturing) and cross-border governance.
* **[European Commission: GPAI Code of Practice](https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/contents-code-gpai)**
    * *Key Insight:* The voluntary compliance layer for systemic risk in General Purpose AI models.

## 🔍 III. National Implementation & Expert Analysis
*Case studies and legal deep-dives into how the AI Act is being operationalized.*

* **[Poland's Centralised AI Authority: KRiBSI Case Study](./data/EU_Sources/poland-ai-act-implementation.pdf)**
    * *Key Insight:* Detailed policy brief on Poland's unique centralized enforcement model vs. the fragmented models of other Member States.
* **[Technical Standardisation & Implementation Challenges](./data/EU_Sources/European_AI_Standards_-_Technical_Standardisation_.pdf)**
    * *Key Insight:* Examination of the "Standards Bottleneck" preventing full enforcement of Human-in-the-loop requirements.
* **[Practitioner's Guide: Bird & Bird AI Act Summary (Feb 2026)](./data/EU_Sources/European_Union_Artificial_Intelligence_Act_Guide.pdf)**
    * *Key Insight:* Comprehensive breakdown of compliance timelines and territorial scope for non-EU providers.
