### 🔍 Key 2026 Findings:
* **India:** The Feb 2026 update now formally defines **SGI** (Synthetically Generated Information), making platforms strictly liable if AI content isn't labeled.
* **EU:** Guidelines published in Feb 2026 clarify that while AI isn't a "person," it can be a "high-risk entity" requiring human-in-the-loop oversight.
* **Russia:** Deep focus on "Digital Sovereignty" means liability is tied to whether the AI is certified for national security interests.
