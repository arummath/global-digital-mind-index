# 🇷🇺 vs 🇮🇳 Digital Sovereignty & AI Liability

Status: Preliminary Analysis. Currently undergoing formal literature review and verification against 2026 legislative updates.

### 1. The Russian "State-Vetted" Model
* **Philosophy:** AI is a tool for national technological sovereignty.
* **Liability:** Focus is on "Certified Systems." If an AI is used in critical infrastructure, the developer and the state-approving body share oversight.
* **2026 Status:** Discussions are moving toward "Digital Agents" having a limited legal status to facilitate B2B transactions.

### 2. The Indian "Digital Public Infrastructure" Model
* **Philosophy:** AI as a layer for social inclusion and economic scale.
* **Liability:** Under the 2026 IT Rules, "Significant Social Media Intermediaries" are strictly liable for AI-generated misinformation (SGI) if not watermarked.
* **2026 Status:** India uses a "Pragmatic Personhood"—treating AI as a "Legal Entity" similar to how Hindu idols or corporations have been treated in past court rulings.
